# Topic: What is virtualization?

**Domain:** VMware

**Granularity:** Foundational concept (will likely be lesson 01 of vSphere fundamentals course)

**Parent topic:** None — this is the foundation

**Prerequisites learner is assumed to have:**
- General computing literacy (knows what an OS is, what RAM and CPU are)
- No virtualization experience whatsoever

**Moments of confusion to resolve:**
- Why virtualization exists at all (what specific problem does it solve?)
- The difference between a physical server and a VM
- Why you would run multiple VMs on one physical machine
- The role of the hypervisor

**Notes:**
- This is the lesson everything else in the course builds on. The analogy chosen here will be referenced in later lessons. Pick the strongest possible analogy — generate at least five candidates per QUALITY.md.
- The before/after is critical here — most learners have never seen the pre-virtualization world.
- The animation should show physical servers vs. virtualized hosts side by side, ideally with a "before/after" mode toggle.
